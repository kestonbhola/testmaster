Welcome to Grenada

Grenada, known as the "Island of Spice," is a beautiful Caribbean country famous for its nutmeg and stunning beaches.

Visit Grand Anse Beach, Annandale Falls, and the historic Fort George for breathtaking views and cultural experiences.

Grenada's cuisine is a mix of African, Indian, and European influences, with dishes like oil down, roti, and fresh seafood.
